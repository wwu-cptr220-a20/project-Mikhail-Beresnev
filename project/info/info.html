  • The concept of the website is to allow anonymous conversations to happen with no lasting documentation. Codes are assigned to a specific chat room through the use of a dictionary (JavaScript). The system will track the conversations for the duration of it's exsistence, after which it will be deleted. At no point will the system track the users, with the exception of the username used to make a message.
